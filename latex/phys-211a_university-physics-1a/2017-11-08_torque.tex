\section*{Torque}
Torque is the rotational analog of force.
\[ \vec{\tau} = \vec{r}\times\vec{F} \]
\( \vec{r} \) is the displacement vector that points from the reference point to
the location of force \( \vec{F} \).
\[ \tau = rF\sin\theta \]
\[ \vec{\tau}_{net} = I\vec{\alpha} \]
